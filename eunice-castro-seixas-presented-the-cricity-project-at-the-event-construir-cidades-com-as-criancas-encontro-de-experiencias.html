18/11/2019

Eunice Castro Seixas presented the CRiCity project at the event “Construir Cidades com as Crianças, Encontro de Experiências”

This event, which took place in the University of Aveiro, Portugal, 15 November 2019., organized by Rosa Madeira, Inês Barbosa (CRiCity fellow) and José Carlos Mota, aimed to promote reflection and debate on the theme of the involvement of children and young people in the enjoyment and construction of the city.

This was done through the sharing of experiences and data from academic, associative and civil society spheres, with the ultimate aim of creating a network among the different actors working on this issue.

The program can be consulted at the following page:
https://www.facebook.com/events/2375467752529322/
