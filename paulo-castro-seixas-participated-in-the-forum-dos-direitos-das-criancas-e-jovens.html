10/11/2019

Paulo Castro Seixas participated in the “Fórum dos Direitos das Crianças e Jovens"

This Forum, which took place at Campus Nova SBE Carcavelos, 4-5 November, 2019., is part of Cascais Child-Friendly City Program and the event itself started with the signature of the protocol "Cascais Child-Friendly City" between the Cascais City Council and the Portuguese Committee for UNICEF. 

The event is part of the Commemoration of the 30th anniversary of the Convention on the Rights of the Child.

Paulo Castro Seixas was an invited speaker to share practices on the CRiCity collaboration with ‘Cascais Child-Friendly City’ activities and also to participate in a conversation about “Right to public space: Experiences and planning natural and built environments”.
