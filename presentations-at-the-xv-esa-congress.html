Presentations at the XV ESA Congress

CRiCity Researchers presented their findings in the 15th Conference of the European Sociological Association, “Sociological Knowledges for Alternative Futures”, held in Barcelona, Spain (online), from 31 August – 3 September 2021.

Covid-19 Pandemic and Children’s Subjective Experiences during Lockdown and Post-Lockdown
Authors: Eunice Castro Seixas, Sara Gonzalez and Paulo Castro Seixas
Submission Type/Conference Track: JS_RN04_RN16 – The effects of Covid-19 Crisis on Children and Young People
Session Details:  JS_RN04_RN16_T05; Time: Thursday, 02 September 2021: 9:00am – 10:30am

Children’s Practices on Urban Parks in Times of Covid-19
Authors: João Teixeira Lopes, Lígia Ferro, Júlia Rodrigues and Eunice Castro Seixas
Submission Type/Conference Track: RN37: Urban Sociology
Session Details: RN37_T01_01; Time: Wednesday, 01 September 2021: 10:45am - 12:15 am
