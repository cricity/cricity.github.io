CRiCity at 14th Conference of the European Sociological Association (ESA)

CRiCity researchers have participated in the 14th Conference of the European Sociological Association (ESA) “Europe and Beyond: Boundaries, Barriers and Belonging” in Manchester, United Kingdom, 20 – 23 August 2019, with the following papers: 

Presentation at Ciência 2019 - Science and Technology in Portugal Summit

Eunice Castro Seixas and Paulo Castro Seixas participated in the the Ciência 2019 - Science and Technology in Portugal Summit, on 8nd July, 2019, which took place at the Lisbon Congress Centre.

Presentation at the 26th APDR Congress in Aveiro,Portugal

Paulo Castro Seixas, Eunice Castro Seixas and Inês Barbosa presented papers at the 26th APDR (Portuguese Association for Regional Development / Associação Portuguesa para o Desenvolvimento Regional) Congress ( “Evidence-based territorial policymaking: formulation, implementation and evaluation of policy”, held in the University of Aveiro (Portugal), 4-5 July 2019.

Presentation at Nordic Geographers Meeting in Trondheim, Norway

Eunice Castro Seixas presented the paper – Exploring children’s place-making processes and designing child-friendly cities through participatory and relational planning in the session “Researching public space: from place-based to process-oriented investigations”, on the 8th Nordic Geographers Meeting taking place in Trondheim, Norway, from the 16th to the 19th of June, 2019.

Cycle Of Movie Projections And Debates On Children’s Rights

With regard to the celebration of the 60th anniversary of the Universal Declaration of the Rights of the Child and the 30 years of the Convention, the CRiCity team of Porto (João Teixeira Lopes, Lígia Ferro e Inês Barbosa) organized a cycle of cinema and debates on Children’s Rights.
