Paulo Castro Seixas participated in the “Fórum dos Direitos das Crianças e Jovens"

This Forum, which took place at Campus Nova SBE Carcavelos, 4-5 November, 2019., is part of Cascais Child-Friendly City Program and the event itself started with the signature of the protocol "Cascais Child-Friendly City" between the Cascais City Council and the Portuguese Committee for UNICEF. 

Workshop "Children's public spaces: Islands of freedom in the city?" (Updated)

CRiCity is organizing the workshop "Children's public spaces: Islands of freedom in the city?" to be held on the 25 October, 2019 at the Lisbon School for Management and Economy (ISEG), in Lisbon, Portugal. The workshop will take place between 9:30 and 18:00 at room 101 in the building "Novo Quelhas". Entrance is free.

RIGHT TO CHILDHOOD - Cycle of Movie Projections and Debates on Children’s Rights

The cycle of cinema projections and debates on Children’s Rights organized by the CRiCity team of Porto, will continue, with a new session with Catarina Tomás, Eunice Seixas and Manuel Sarmento, dedicated to the RIGHT TO CHILDHOOD. This event will take place at the Faculty of Arts and Humanities of the University of Porto, at Anfiteatro Nobre, on October 18th at 17:30. The session is open to all and participation is free.
